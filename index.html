Locating your Canvas token
⚠ Treat this token as a secure password. It lives strictly in ephemeral memory.
1
Log in to your institution's portal: canvas.anu.edu.au
2
Open Account Settings from the top-left navigation drawer.
3
Scroll down to the Approved Integrations section and click + New Access Token.
4
Add a purpose description and set an expiration date for your academic cycle.
5
Click Generate Token and paste it directly into the application.
